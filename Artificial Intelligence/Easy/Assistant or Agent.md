## Solution

The core of this CTF is to know what is the difference between ai assistant and ai agent. Where they explain that `AI assistants` are reactive tools that wait for your prompts to perform specific tasks like answering questions or drafting text. `AI agents` are goal-driven systems that independently plan, make decisions, and execute multi-step workflows using external tools without needing constant human intervention.

They give you scenes and you have to choose between `Assistant or Agent`.
